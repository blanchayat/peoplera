Privacy & GDPR

Privacy Policy

Peoplera is built to be privacy-first. This page explains what we collect, how we process it, and the rights you have under GDPR.

Contact
For privacy requests, email privacy@peoplera.app.

What data we collect

We collect your email address for authentication and account access. We also collect usage data (for example, feature interactions and basic performance metrics) to improve the product. Uploaded CVs and PDFs are processed to generate outputs inside the product, but are not permanently stored.

How data is stored

Peoplera uses Supabase to store user and product data. Data is encrypted at rest. Our data hosting is configured for EU region storage.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to deliver Peoplera:
Supabase (authentication and database)
Anthropic Claude API (AI processing)
Stripe (payments)
Google OAuth (sign-in)

User rights

Under GDPR, you can request access, deletion, and portability of your personal data. To submit a request, email privacy@peoplera.app.

Cookie policy

Peoplera uses session cookies only to maintain authentication state. We do not use tracking cookies.
